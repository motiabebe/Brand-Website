Frequently Asked Questions

What is the shipping policy?

Shipping is free for all orders over $50. For orders under $50, there is a flat rate shipping fee of $5.99.

All phones come with a 1 year warranty. You can also purchase an extended warranty for $99.

What is the return policy?

You can return your phone within 30 days of purchase for a full refund.
